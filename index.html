ABOUT LIGHTHOUSE

We at LIGHTHOUSE have created a one stop solution for students like you to find a scholarship that caters to your needs and provides you all the monetary help that you need. Scholarships for academic instituions from all around the world are listed here for you to choose the best fit for you. The data listed here is fetched from the entire world wide web using a web data scraper and further utilised to provide intelligent recommendations to the student using cloud computing technology. Lighthouse is here to light the way to your perfect scholarship. Happy scholarship finding!!!
